NYC Parking Tickets

Ever been curious about how parking is enforced in the Big Apple? You've come to the right place! With over 9 million unique parking tickets, this database captures an entire year's worth of parking violations from NYC. Feel free to search tickets by summons number, upload your own ticket or vehicle information, delete a ticket (unfortunately you still have to pay the ticket ;) ), or check out our collection of interesting parking statistics!
